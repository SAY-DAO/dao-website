SAY

SAY is a startup with the focus of social responsibilities, where children who are under the care of NGOs can have virtual families to take care of all the daily, educational and developmental needs.
SAY tracks all donations to their intended destination over a transparent platform by creating a network similar to SWIFT and consisting of the following data: Donor/Bank/NGO/Social Worker/Child/Need which helps users to not feel skeptical toward the result of their donations.
