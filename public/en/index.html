Why SAY

To have financial transparency

To make things happen

With the help of professional social workers, we came up with a specific list of needs to cover all fundamental requirements for a child to grow up in a stable environment.

To protect children's rights

We believe in the quality of childhood, and we defend the rights of every single child, following the UNCRC. (Convention on the Rights of the Child.)

To contribute in social responsibilities

People can now choose ways of engaging with a child's life. Every interaction with the app makes an impact on a real child.

To have FINANCIAL TRANSPARENCY

Every single transaction made with any NGO for any child, will and can be traceable through our Blockchain technology.

To feel even more closer (This feature is not available)

With the help of SAY Brain, you can adopt a child with a similar character and personality traits as your own. This bond will be the key to building yourself and others a better life.

To virtually touch a heart

Users now have the chance to create virtual families for real children. Families big enough to nourish a feeling, a belief, and a plan.
